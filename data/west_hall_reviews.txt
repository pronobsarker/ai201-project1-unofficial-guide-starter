DORM: West Hall
SOURCE: Roomsurf, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: While West Hall is part of the modern Quarry Suites complex, its specific physical location is notably far from central classes and dining.

REVIEW 2: The interior layout is highly comfortable, featuring carpeted living areas, built-in closets, and split multi-zone bathrooms.

REVIEW 3: Factual records confirm that West Hall holds 43 students across two floors, with central air conditioning and a shared first-floor oven.

PROS:
Extremely quiet and peaceful study environment due to its secluded location
- Modern split-bathroom layout and carpeted living spaces
Reliable central air conditioning

CONS:
Longest walk to central campus among the Quarry Suites
Kitchenettes lack in-apartment ovens or stoves
- Lacks elevator access

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen prioritizing a quiet, modern, and comfortable apartment who do not mind a longer walk to classes
