DORM: Writing House
SOURCE: Gettysburg College English Department, The Gettysburgian

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student reviews were found. Officially, the Writing House is a residential program for students passionate about journalism, poetry, and creative writing.

REVIEW 2: The house accommodates fifteen students across three floors, hosting creative readings, author dinners, and cultural trips.

REVIEW 3: Residents have expressed distress regarding the conversion of Smith House into senior-only Independent Living Communities, which displaces their thematic home.

PROS:
Fantastic creative environment with academic dinners and professional writer workshops
- Beautiful physical house layout with five single bedrooms and a full kitchen

CONS:
Displaced from its historical home due to the senior pilot program
- Extremely small capacity of fifteen students

BEST FOR: Creative writers, poets, and journalists seeking an inspiring, quiet, and collaborative haven
