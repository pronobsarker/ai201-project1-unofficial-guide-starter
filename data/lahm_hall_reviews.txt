DORM: Lahm Hall
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Structured Data, Civil War Institute

REVIEW 1: Lahm Hall offers an exceptionally quiet, low-density living space, housing only 24 upperclass students in a converted motel configuration.

REVIEW 2: Every single and double room contains its own private bathroom, tiled flooring, and a dedicated climate control unit.

REVIEW 3: The lack of on-site laundry is a minor inconvenience, as residents must walk to the neighboring Albaugh House to wash their clothes.

PROS:
High level of privacy with an in-suite gender-neutral bathroom
Individual dedicated climate controls
- Private parking lot and peaceful, quiet environment

CONS:
No on-site laundry facilities (must use Albaugh House)
No kitchen facilities inside the building
Significant walking distance to central campus academic buildings

BEST FOR: Upperclass students who prioritize privacy and quiet, and do not mind off-site laundry or a longer walk
