DORM: Farm House
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Houses Directory, The Gettysburgian

REVIEW 1: The mission of the house is highly collaborative, focusing on sustainable living, environmental consciousness, and direct community engagement.

REVIEW 2: Residents host phenomenal collaborative events, such as massive live-music Halloween parties in partnership with Art House.

REVIEW 3: The administrative decision to relocate Farm House from Corner Cottage to Bregenzer House has caused significant resident frustration and anxiety.

PROS:
Excellent community for students passionate about sustainable living
Hosts popular, large-scale social events with live student music
Relocating to Bregenzer House for 2026-2027 to continue its sustainable mission

CONS:
Displaced from its historical home at Corner Cottage
Relocation process caused friction with housing administrators

BEST FOR: Eco-conscious students looking to practice and educate others on sustainable lifestyles
