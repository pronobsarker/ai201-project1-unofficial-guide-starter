DORM: Blue Note House
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Houses Directory, The Gettysburgian

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student forum reviews were found. Officially, Blue Note House is a dedicated community of students who perform and appreciate jazz music.

REVIEW 2: The house works closely with the Sunderman Conservatory of Music to host live jazz performances, guest lecturers, and structured music events.

REVIEW 3: The recent administrative decision to relocate the house from Lau House and merge it with Harmony House at 209 N. Washington has caused significant resident frustration.

PROS:
Unparalleled integration with the campus jazz scene and Sunderman Conservatory
Relocating to spacious apartment units at 209 N. Washington Street
- Outstanding opportunities to host live musical social events

CONS:
Relocation and merging has caused stress and a feeling of a lack of control
- Highly selective application process

BEST FOR: Jazz musicians and conservatory students wanting to integrate music into their social living spaces
