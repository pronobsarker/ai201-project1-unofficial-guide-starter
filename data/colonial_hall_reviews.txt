DORM: Colonial Hall
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data, Civil War Institute

REVIEW 1: Colonial Hall is a converted commercial motel situated on the east side of campus, representing the furthest dormitory from central academic venues.

REVIEW 2: The layout is highly valued because every double suite features a private bathroom, dedicated climate controls, and easy parking.

REVIEW 3: While the walk to central classes is long, the location is highly advantageous for students who frequently walk to the downtown shops and restaurants.

PROS:
Private, gender-neutral bathroom inside every suite
Dedicated individual heating and air conditioning units
- Private parking lot and close proximity to downtown Gettysburg

CONS:
Furthest dormitory from central campus academic buildings and dining halls
- Lacks any on-site kitchen facilities
- No elevator access to the second-level rooms
Note: Officially announced as unavailable for the 2026-2027 year

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen who want highly independent, motel-style living with maximum bathroom privacy
