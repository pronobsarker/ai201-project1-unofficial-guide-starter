REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student forum reviews were found. Officially, Corkran Hall serves as the on-campus STEM House, accommodating 43 science-focused students.

PROS:
Highly collaborative community for science and research majors
Multi-zone bathroom design optimizes convenience for multiple residents
On-site laundry on the second floor and common kitchen on the first floor

CONS:
In-apartment kitchenettes lack full stoves or ovens
Requires a partial campus meal plan
- No elevator access

BEST FOR: STEM majors seeking a comfortable, collaborative, and modern apartment layout
