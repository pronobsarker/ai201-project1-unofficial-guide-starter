DORM: Penn Hall (Pennsylvania Hall)
SOURCE: Cupola, Wikipedia, Reddit

REVIEW 1: Students describe Penn Hall as iconic, calling it the literal centerpiece and defining architectural landmark of the entire campus.

REVIEW 2: The structure holds deep historical value, having been occupied by both Union and Confederate forces as a lookout and hospital during the Battle of Gettysburg.

PROS:
Iconic architectural style that acts as the centerpiece of campus
Deeply historic and structurally majestic landmark

CONS:
Heavily rumored to experience paranormal and ghostly activities

BEST FOR: History buffs and students who value living near iconic landmark structures
