DORM: Apple Hall
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Apple Hall is named in honor of John Apple, but we simply refer to it as Apple. It houses approximately 200 students near the CUB, Dining Center, and Jaeger Center.

REVIEW 2: The third floor features fantastic loft-style apartments with internal wooden staircases leading to elevated sleeping levels, offering a highly unique layout.

REVIEW 3: While the location is unbeatable, the basement laundry room has been subject to maintenance complaints, and there was even an April Fools joke about a raccoon being found down there.

PROS:
Spectacular central location next to dining, recreation, and class halls
- Highly unique third-floor loft apartments with internal staircases
- Full in-apartment kitchens and zero mandatory meal plans

CONS:
Lacks elevator access and is only partially ADA accessible
Basement laundry area is prone to maintenance and humidity issues

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen seeking a highly central, independent apartment with full cooking capabilities
