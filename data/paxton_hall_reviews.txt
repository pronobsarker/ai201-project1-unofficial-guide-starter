DORM: Paxton Hall
SOURCE: Roomsurf, The Gettysburgian, Civil War Institute

REVIEW 1: Paxton Hall is arguably the highest quality dorm option, with its converted motel structure offering private bathrooms and excellent noise insulation.

REVIEW 2: The building is located on the east periphery of campus, making it quiet and peaceful as it is removed from the noisy fraternity rows.

REVIEW 3: While the distance to central dining can be frustrating in winter, having a private bathroom and an individual AC unit makes it exceptionally comfortable.

PROS:
Private gender-neutral bathroom inside every room
- Excellent noise isolation and quiet study environment
Individual climate control and dedicated on-site parking

CONS:
Removed from the center of campus, requiring a longer daily walk
- Lacks an elevator or indoor corridors

BEST FOR: Upperclass students seeking highly quiet, independent living with a private bathroom
