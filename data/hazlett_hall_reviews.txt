DORM: Hazlett Hall
SOURCE: Roomsurf, Civil War Institute

REVIEW 1: Hazlett is a fantastic Quarry apartment choice, featuring extremely clean, modern bedrooms with large windows that provide abundant natural light.

REVIEW 2: The apartments only feature kitchenettes rather than full kitchens, which is a minor limitation, but having a partial meal plan offsets this issue.

REVIEW 3: Factual logs show that Hazlett contains carpeted common areas and semi-private, multi-zone bathrooms, making it highly comfortable.

PROS:
Modernised, exceptionally clean design with excellent natural lighting
- Highly comfortable carpeted living rooms and private bedrooms
- Fully air-conditioned with modern apartment furniture

CONS:
Lack of in-apartment ovens or stoves
Mandatory partial campus meal plan

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen who want a clean, well-lit, and modern apartment on the northwest side of campus
