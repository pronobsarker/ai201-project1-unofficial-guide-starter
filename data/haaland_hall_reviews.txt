DORM: Haaland Hall
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Haaland Hall is a nice, highly modernised student apartment building that offers beautiful views of Quarry Pond on the northwest side of campus.

REVIEW 2: The apartment suites are spacious and comfortable, featuring central air conditioning, carpeted living rooms, and convenient built-in closets.

REVIEW 3: Factual logs show that Haaland contains an elevator and is fully ADA-compliant, making it highly accessible for all students.

PROS:
Beautiful views of Quarry Pond in a quiet northwest campus location
Elevator access and full central air conditioning
Multi-zone bathroom design and on-site laundry on the third floor

CONS:
Kitchenettes lack in-unit stoves and ovens (common kitchen on second floor)
Mandatory partial campus meal plan required

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen seeking modern, scenic, and fully accessible apartment-style living
