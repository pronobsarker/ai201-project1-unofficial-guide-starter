DORM: Diaspora House
SOURCE: Roomsurf, The Gettysburgian, Office of Multicultural Engagement

REVIEW 1: Diaspora House is an exceptional residential space that allows students of colour to connect, advocate, and host amazing social events.

REVIEW 2: The extended community around D-House and the Black Student Union is highly valued for making everyone feel included while celebrating their individuality.

REVIEW 3: Officially, the house works closely with the Office of Multicultural Engagement to foster cultural diversity and awareness of the African diaspora.

PROS:
Vital, highly supportive cultural community for BIPOC students and allies
- Strong collaborative ties with the Office of Multicultural Engagement
- Outstanding social atmosphere and highly rated campus events

CONS:
Prone to typical noise carrying due to older physical structures
- Highly limited residential capacity relative to campus demand

BEST FOR: BIPOC students and allies committed to multicultural awareness and community advocacy
