DORM: Musselman Hall
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Musselman Hall is situated in a premium central location right next to 'Muss Beach' and the library, making it highly coveted by sophomores and juniors.

REVIEW 2: The rooms are standard co-ed doubles with built-in closets, but the selection process during the housing lottery is incredibly stressful due to high demand.

REVIEW 3: The physical climate of the building can get uncomfortably hot, particularly on the fourth floor, which has historically suffered from overheating and ventilation issues.

PROS:
Exceptional central location adjacent to main campus social lawns and the library
- Equipped with reliable central air conditioning and standard microfridges
- Full kitchen and laundry access situated on the first floor

CONS:
Lacks an elevator across four residential levels
- Prone to severe overheating on the upper floors during winter and autumn
- Highly competitive to secure during the sophomore housing lottery

BEST FOR: Sophomores and juniors seeking standard traditional housing in the absolute center of campus
