DORM: Hillel House
SOURCE: Gettysburg College News, Hillel.org, The Gettysburgian

REVIEW 1: Becoming the house leader of the Hillel House is an incredible leadership experience, offering a fantastic opportunity to host community events.

REVIEW 2: The house offers a warm, welcoming home for Jewish students and allies to enjoy Jewish movies, kosher food, and Shabbat dinners.

REVIEW 3: The house is highly celebrated for its Challah for Hunger program where students bake and sell challah to raise money for charity, but residents are adjusting to its relocation to Ice House G.

PROS:
Exceptional religious and cultural community for Jewish students and allies
Famous campus-wide charity events like Challah for Hunger
Relocating to premium townhouse spaces in the Ice House Complex

CONS:
Relocation has caused transitional stress and adjustment anxiety

BEST FOR: Jewish students and allies seeking an active, welcoming community focused on faith and service
