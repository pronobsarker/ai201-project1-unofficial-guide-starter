DORM: Humor House
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Houses Directory, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student forum reviews were found. Officially, Humor House provides a lighthearted, fun, and highly positive atmosphere to students of all backgrounds.

REVIEW 2: The house serves as the official on-campus headquarters for the Gettysburg Improv Club and the Shots in the Dark show troupe.

REVIEW 3: Located in the 209 N. Washington Street apartments, the house layout is highly comfortable, featuring full kitchens and modern living spaces.

PROS:
Extremely fun, cheerful, and expressive social environment
- Strong structural integration with the campus improv comedy scene
Comfortable apartment layouts with full kitchens and central AC

CONS:
High-energy and loud atmosphere may not suit quiet-seeking students
Sharing apartments with up to eight students can lead to minor noise

BEST FOR: Comedians, performance artists, and students seeking a highly expressive, cheerful social home
