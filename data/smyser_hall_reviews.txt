DORM: Smyser Hall
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student forum reviews were found. Officially, Smyser Hall is a converted motel on the east side of campus housing 35 students in 19 highly independent rooms.

REVIEW 2: Unlike other motel conversions, Smyser features a combined laundry, kitchen, and common area on-site, offering excellent self-sufficiency.

REVIEW 3: Factual records verify that each tiled room is equipped with its own private bathroom, dedicated climate control, and a standard MicroFridge.

PROS:
In-room private bathroom and dedicated climate controls
On-site laundry, kitchen, and common area
- Extremely quiet and low-density residential environment

CONS:
Geographically isolated on the far east side of campus
- Lacks elevator access and is only partially ADA accessible

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen who want motel-style privacy with the added convenience of on-site cooking and laundry
