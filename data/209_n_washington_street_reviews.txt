DORM: 209 N. Washington Street
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student reviews were found. Officially, this complex houses 31 upperclass students across five highly independent apartments.

REVIEW 2: Factual records confirm that each unit features a full kitchen, comfortable dining areas, and carpeted rooms with adjustable wooden beds.

REVIEW 3: Unlike other off-center apartment complexes, 209 N. Washington Street features a dedicated laundry room located directly on the first floor.

PROS:
Premium location close to Schmucker Hall and central classes
On-site first-floor laundry facilities
- Fully equipped kitchens with no mandatory campus meal plan

CONS:
Large apartment groupings (up to eight students) can cause internal noise
- Lacks elevator access or ADA-compliant features

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen wanting a highly central, independent apartment with in-building laundry and full kitchen freedom
