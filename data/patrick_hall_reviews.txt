DORM: Patrick Hall
SOURCE: Roomsurf, The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: The bedrooms are consistently comfortable, featuring durable tiled floors, individual microfridges, and reliable central air conditioning.

REVIEW 2: Fostered by the First-Year Seminar housing model, Patrick Hall facilitates a highly integrated learning community where academic discussions naturally flow into the shared common spaces.

REVIEW 3: The random roommate pairing and community-building initiatives, such as competitive field days and floor forums, help make Patrick Hall feel like a welcoming home early in the academic year.

PROS:
Excellent peer-to-peer social bonds due to seminar-based placement
Standard in-room MicroFridges and full air conditioning
- Highly central on-site laundry and communal kitchen on the first floor

CONS:
Complete lack of ADA accessibility and no elevator access across four floors
- Communal, single-gender bathroom configurations

BEST FOR: First-year students who want a traditional, academically integrated, and highly social dorm experience
