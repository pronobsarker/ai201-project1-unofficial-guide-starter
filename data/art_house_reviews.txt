DORM: Art House
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Houses Directory

REVIEW 1: Art House serves as an incredible student-led community where creative students of all academic majors can collaborate and feel welcomed.

REVIEW 2: The house leader does an exceptional job making the space feel inviting, hosting amazing collaborative events like live concerts and art exhibitions.

REVIEW 3: Living in Art House provides a fantastic social alternative to Greek life, allowing students to integrate their artistic passions directly into their social lives.

PROS:
Exceptionally inclusive, positive, and collaborative creative environment
- Outstanding on-campus social events featuring live music from student bands
- Relocated to Hutchinson House for 2026-2027, preserving its close-knit model

CONS:
Extremely small capacity of around ten students, making it highly selective
Subject to periodic administrative relocation

BEST FOR: Creative-minded students and art appreciators seeking an inclusive, collaborative community
