DORM: RISE House
SOURCE: Upperclass RISE Directory, Gettysburg College Structured Data

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student reviews were found. Officially, RISE stands for Respecting Individual Student Expectations, offering a completely substance-free home at 227 Carlisle Street.

REVIEW 2: Residents commit to a lifestyle entirely free of alcohol and tobacco, while adhering to strict, 24-hour quiet hours.

REVIEW 3: The house organizes wonderful alternative social programming, including trips to Hershey Park, laser tag, and weekly board game nights.

PROS:
Guaranteed substance-free environment with strict 24-hour quiet hours
- Outstanding alternative social programming open to the entire campus
- Exceptionally quiet, clean, and peaceful study environment

CONS:
Strict behavioral guidelines are incompatible with students seeking a typical weekend social scene
Located slightly off-center on Carlisle Street

BEST FOR: Students committed to a clean, quiet, and strictly substance-free residential experience with active alternative social options
