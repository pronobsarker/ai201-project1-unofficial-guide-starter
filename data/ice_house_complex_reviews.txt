DORM: Ice House Complex
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Structured Data, Historic Marker Database

REVIEW 1: Ice House isn't an old-timey freezer; it's a historic upperclass townhouse option with a fascinating industrial past converted into student housing.

REVIEW 2: The Ice House G townhouses are massive three-story, six-person units featuring three double bedrooms, full kitchens, and two full bathrooms.

REVIEW 3: While the buildings are highly comfortable, we have experienced some maintenance issues, such as stovetop ventilation failures and localized heating issues.

PROS:
Massive, highly independent multi-story townhouse layouts
- Features full kitchens, multiple bathrooms, and central air conditioning
- No campus meal plan required
- Rich historical aesthetic and excellent proximity to classes

CONS:
No laundry facilities inside the buildings (must wash off-site)
Prone to minor heating and stove ventilation maintenance issues
Multi-floor stairs lack elevators and limit physical accessibility

BEST FOR: Upperclassmen wanting highly spacious, historic townhouse living in close proximity to classes
