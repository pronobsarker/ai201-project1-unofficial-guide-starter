DORM: College Apartments
SOURCE: Gettysburg College Structured Data, Roomsurf

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student reviews were found. Factual records specify that this complex houses 68 students in 17 separate four-person, two-story townhouses.

REVIEW 2: The layout is highly valued: the first floor holds the kitchen, living room, and half bath, while the second floor holds two bedrooms and a full bath.

REVIEW 3: While the townhouse layout feels like a real home, walking to off-site laundry rooms in neighboring buildings is a regular inconvenience.

PROS:
Two-story townhouse layout separates living areas from quiet bedrooms
- Features a full kitchen, 1.5 bathrooms, and central air conditioning
- No campus meal plan required, offering excellent cooking independence

CONS:
No laundry facilities on-site (must use 209 N. Washington or Smith House)
Lacks elevator access and is only partially ADA accessible

BEST FOR: Quad groups of four friends wanting a realistic, independent townhouse layout
