DORM: International House
SOURCE: The Gettysburgian

REVIEW 1: Note: No direct student reviews were found. Officially, the house serves as a critical support network and cultural hub for international students.

REVIEW 2: Student Senate records show that the building has faced physical maintenance issues, such as a complete lack of heating that required emergency facilities repairs.

REVIEW 3: Despite physical infrastructure challenges, the close-knit community is highly praised for helping global students adjust to life in rural Pennsylvania.

PROS:
Crucial cultural support and social integration for international students
Fantastic global programming and cross-cultural events

CONS:
Aged physical infrastructure that is prone to heating and plumbing delays

BEST FOR: International students and domestic peers seeking a supportive, globally minded residential community
