DORM: Stine Hall
SOURCE: Roomsurf, The Gettysburgian, Gettysburg College Residential Education

REVIEW 1: Stine is positioned in the premier first-year location, offering immediate proximity to the library and dining facilities alongside spectacular sunset views over the campus.

REVIEW 2: While thin walls can occasionally let noise travel between adjacent rooms, the location and physical comfort of the building make it highly desirable.

REVIEW 3: The late-2025 renovations have completely transformed the building, introducing beautiful new study rooms, a fully equipped communal kitchen, and modern single-stall private bathrooms.

PROS:
Fully modernised in 2025 with brand-new furniture, study lounges, and a kitchen
Single-stall private bathrooms offer exceptional privacy and comfort
- Highly advanced heating and cooling systems installed
- Immediate access to Musselman Library and the Dining Center

CONS:
Internal wall insulation remains thin, allowing sound to carry
- High demand makes it exceptionally competitive to secure

BEST FOR: First-year students who desire modern, state-of-the-art facilities, excellent privacy, and a highly central social location
